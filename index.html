WELCOME TO
MIDNIGHT MOON TATTOO

At Midnight Moon Tattoo we celebrate the artistry and significance behind tattoos. Nestled in the serene landscapes of Meredith, New Hampshire, along the shores of Lake Winnipesaukee, our studio stands as a beacon of creativity and expression.

Our APP certified piercers use only implant-grade fine jewelry, ensuring both beauty and safety. Every client is treated with respect, care, and artistic dedication. We specialize in custom tattooing, fine jewelry curation, and precision body piercing in a pristine environment.
